Content Creation Days

Starting from £30 per hour, content creation days are designed to elevate your brand with high-quality, strategic visuals that feel premium, polished, and commercially effective.

Each content day is planned with intention from the start. Before shooting, I create a moodboard, discuss your goals, and develop ideas around your brand so the final content feels cohesive, elevated, and aligned with what works across social media.

On the day, I can capture exactly what you need — from behind-the-scenes moments to treatments, products, campaigns, team content, lifestyle visuals, and branded footage. I also guide the process creatively, helping shape ideas around what is most visually effective and engaging for your audience.

After your shoot, you’ll receive a shared drive filled with raw videos and photography, giving you a full bank of premium content to use across your social platforms, website, launches, campaigns, and wider marketing.

Where needed, I can then transform that content into professionally edited reels, social videos, and refined imagery, giving you polished assets that are ready to post and built to strengthen your brand presence.

Bespoke packages are available depending on the scale of content needed.
