Content Creation Days

Starting from £30 per hour, content creation days are designed to give businesses a polished bank of professional visuals that can be used confidently across social media, websites, launches, campaigns, and wider marketing.

Each content day is planned with intention before shooting even begins. The process starts by discussing a brand’s goals, shaping ideas around what it needs, and creating a moodboard so the final visuals feel cohesive, elevated, and aligned with what works best across social media.

On the day, content can be captured around exactly what a business needs, whether that is behind-the-scenes footage, treatments, products, team content, lifestyle visuals, campaign imagery, branded photography, or video footage. Creative direction is also offered throughout, helping shape ideas around what is most visually effective and engaging for the intended audience.

After the shoot, clients receive a shared drive filled with raw videos and photography, giving them a strong bank of content to use across their platforms, website, launches, and wider marketing.

Where needed, that content can then be turned into professionally edited reels, refined imagery, and polished visual assets that are ready to post and designed to strengthen overall brand presence.

Bespoke packages are available depending on how much content is needed and the type of output required from the day.
